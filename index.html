Forklift Solutions deliver a professional service at the right price. Our aim is to provide the best value for money forklift hire Newcastle & Sunderland has, without compromising on quality. We are based in the North East, with only qualified technicians delivering a prompt and effective service.

As an authorised Still partner we offer competitive rates on new equipment for contract hire, purchase and lease purchase. This includes used forklift equipment from proven ranges from hand pallet trucks to container handlers, available now to hire or purchase.
